VOLUNTEERS

Every Year We Look For Hard Working, Capable Volunteers Who Will Help To Ensure The Smooth Running Of The Festival. We Welcome Applications For Work For A Minimum Of 10 Days Around The Festival, Which Will Include A Full Training Schedule. In Return For Volunteering At The Festival We Offer Accommodation, Meals, Our Sincerest Thank You And An Experience That You Will Never Forget! If You Are Interested In Being A Part Of The FGLF 2019 Team, Please Email Our Volunteer Coordinator Your CV And/Or Up To 200 Words On How You Think You May Be Able To Help.

Volunteer.glf@gmail.com

BENEFACTORS

Support the Fairway Galle Literary Festival! Like many festivals of its kind, the Fairway Galle Literary Festival (FGLF) is delivered by a small team of dedicated individuals and returns year-after-year only by the grace of generous sponsors and contributors. We are always on the lookout for new supporters to add to our growing FGLF family. There are a number of ways to support the festival – whether in cash, kind or air miles – through new schemes launched for benefactors to the festival. We welcome your participation. To find out more about how you can help, please contact our Festival Manager
